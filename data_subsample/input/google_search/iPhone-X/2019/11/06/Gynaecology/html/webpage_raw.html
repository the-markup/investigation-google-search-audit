    Description

    Gynaecology or gynecology is the medical practice dealing with the health of the female reproductive systems and the breasts. Outside medicine, the term means "the science of women". Its counterpart is andrology, which deals with medical issues specific to the male reproductive system. Wikipedia
    Significant diseases: Gynaecological cancers, infertility, dysmenorrhea
    Significant tests: Laparoscopy
    System: Female reproductive system
    Specialist: Gynaecologist
    Subdivisions: Oncology, Maternal medicine, Maternal-fetal medicine
