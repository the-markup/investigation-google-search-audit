    Why is Middletown called Middletown?
    On September 11, 1651, the General Court of Connecticut established the town of "Mattabesett". A couple of years later in November of 1653, the settlement was renamed Middletown. It was chosen because the site was approximate halfway between Windsor and Saybrook on the Great River.
