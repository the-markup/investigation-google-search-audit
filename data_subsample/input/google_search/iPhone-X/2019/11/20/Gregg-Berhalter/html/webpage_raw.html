      Gregg Berhalter (/brhltr/, br-HAWL-tr; born August 1, 1973) is an American retired soccer player and head coach of the United States men's national soccer team. Berhalter previously coached Columbus Crew SC in Major League Soccer, Hammarby IF in Sweden, and served as an assistant coach for the LA Galaxy.
