    What type of bulldog is Mississippi State?
    Bulldog. Mississippi State University athletic teams are called Bulldogs, a name earned and maintained over the decades by the tough, tenacious play by student-athletes wearing the maroon and white. The official school mascot is an American Kennel Club-registered English bulldog given the inherited title of “Bully.”
