    Description

    Butyrate or butanoate is the traditional name for the conjugate base of butyric acid. The formula of the butyrate ion is C 4H 7O 2. The name is used as part of the name of esters and salts of butyric acid Wikipedia
    Chemical formula: C; 4H; 7O; 2
    Molar mass: 87.098 g mol1
    Melting point: -7.9 C
    Classification: Short-chain fatty acid
    Appearance: oily, colorless liquid
    PubChem CID: 104775
