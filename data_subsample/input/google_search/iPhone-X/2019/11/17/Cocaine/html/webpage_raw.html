      Description

      Cocaine, also known as coke, is a strong stimulant most frequently used as a recreational drug. It is commonly snorted, inhaled as smoke, or dissolved and injected into a vein. Mental effects may include loss of contact with reality, an intense feeling of happiness, or agitation. Wikipedia
      Formula: C17H21NO4
      IUPAC ID: methyl (1R,2R,3S,5S)-3- (benzoyloxy)-8-methyl-8-azabicyclo[3.2.1] octane-2-carboxylate
      Molar mass: 303.353 g/mol
      Onset of action: seconds to minutes
      Metabolism: liver CYP3A4
      Elimination half-life: 1 hour
      Solubility in water: 1.8 mg/mL (20 C)
