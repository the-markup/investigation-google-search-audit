      Description

      Pretty Ricky is an American R&B/hip hop group originating from Miami, Florida. The group originally consisted of brothers Ala Diamond Smith and Spectacular Smith, plus Corey Mathis and Marcus Cooper. Pleasure P was the group's main singer while the other three members performed as rappers. Wikipedia
      Origin: Miami, FL (1997)
      Members: Spectacular Blue Smith, Pleasure P, Diamond Blue Smith, Emmanual DeAnda, More
      Record labels: Atlantic Records, Cash Money Records, Bluestar Entertainment, More
      Genres: Contemporary R&B, Hip hop music
