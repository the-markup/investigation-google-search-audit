      Chris Kermode is a retired English male tennis player, a former tournament director and the current executive chairman & president of the Association of Tennis Professionals. On 20 November 2013 Kermode was appointed as the ATP executive chairman & president to succeed Brad Drewett who died of an illness in May 2013. Wikipedia
      Born: January 13, 1965 (age 54 years), Lancashire, United Kingdom
      Country (sports): United Kingdom
