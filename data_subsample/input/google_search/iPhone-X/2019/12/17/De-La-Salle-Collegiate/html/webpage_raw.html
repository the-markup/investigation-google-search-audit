    De La Salle Collegiate High School is an all-boys Catholic high school run by the De La Salle Christian Brothers. Founded in 1926, the school was located on the east side of Detroit before moving to its current location in Warren, Michigan in 1982. Wikipedia
    Principal: Nate Maus
    Colors: Purple, Gold
    Founded: 1926
    Motto: Builders of Boys, Makers of Men
    President: John Knight
    Accreditation: North Central Association of Colleges and Schools
    School type: Private school
