      Description

      Miss America is an annual competition that is open to women from the United States between the ages of 17 and 25. Originating in 1921 as a "bathing beauty revue", the contest is now judged on competitors' talent performances and interviews. Wikipedia
      Founded: September 8, 1921; 98 years ago; Atlantic City, New Jersey, U.S
      Headquarters: Atlantic City, New Jersey, U.S.
      Key people: Shantel Krebs, Gretchen Carlson
