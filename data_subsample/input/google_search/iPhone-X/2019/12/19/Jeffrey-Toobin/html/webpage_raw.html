      Jeffrey Ross Toobin is an American lawyer, blogger, author, pundit, and legal analyst for CNN and The New Yorker. During the IranContra affair, he served as an associate counsel in the Department of Justice, and moved from law into writing during the 1990s. Wikipedia

      Spouse: Amy Bennett McIntosh (m. 1986)
