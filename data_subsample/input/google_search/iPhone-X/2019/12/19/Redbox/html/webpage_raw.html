      Redbox Automated Retail LLC is an American company specializing in DVD, Blu-ray, and 4K UHD rentals via automated retail kiosks. Redbox kiosks feature the company's signature red color and are located at convenience stores, fast food restaurants, grocery stores, mass retailers, and pharmacies. Wikipedia
