    Ampeg is a manufacturer best known for its bass amplifiers. Originally established in 1946 in Linden, New Jersey by Everitt Hull and Stanley Michaels as "Michaels-Hull Electronic Labs," today Ampeg is part of the Yamaha Guitar Group. Wikipedia
    Headquarters: Washington
    Founded: 1946
    Subsidiaries: Dan Armstrong
    Parent organization: Yamaha Guitar Group, Inc.
    Founders: Stanley Michaels, Everett Hull
