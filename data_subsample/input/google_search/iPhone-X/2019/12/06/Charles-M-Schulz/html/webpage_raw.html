      Charles M. Schulz Museum and Research Center
      Museum in Santa Rosa, California
      The Charles M. Schulz Museum and Research Center is a museum dedicated to the works of Charles M. Schulz, creator of the Peanuts comic strip. The museum opened on August 17, 2002, and is in Santa Rosa, California. The museum is home to many of the original Peanuts strips, as well as other artwork by Schulz.
