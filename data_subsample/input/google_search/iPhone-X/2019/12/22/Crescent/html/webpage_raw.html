    crescent
    /kres()nt/
    Learn to pronounce
    noun
    noun: crescent; plural noun: crescents
    1. 1.
      the curved sickle shape of the waxing or waning moon.
      • a representation of a crescent used as an emblem of Islam or of Turkey.
      • historical
        the political power of Islam or of the Ottoman Empire.
        noun: Crescent; noun: the Crescent
    2. 2.
      a thing that has has the shape of a single curve, especially one that is broad in the center and tapers to a point at each end.
      "a three-mile crescent of golden sand"
      amusement
      Informal
      the state or experience of finding something funny.
      "we looked with amusement at our horoscopes"
      full definition
      h
      Similar:
      half-moon
      sickle-shape
      semicircle
      arc
      curve
      bow
      arch
      bend
      crook
      demilune
      lunula
      • British
        a street or row of houses forming an arc.
        "we lived at Westway Crescent"
      • Heraldry
        a charge in the form of a crescent, typically with the points upward (also a mark of cadency for a second son).
    3. 3.
      a moth or butterfly with crescent-shaped markings on the wings.
    adjective
    adjective: crescent
    1. 1.
      having the shape of a crescent.
      "a crescent moon"
    2. 2.
      literary
      growing, increasing, or developing.
    Origin
    late Middle English cressant, from Old French creissant, from Latin crescere grow. The spelling change in the 17th century was due to the influence of the Latin.

    What is the meaning of the crescent moon?
    The crescent symbol is primarily used to represent the Moon, not necessarily in a particular lunar phase. When used to represent a waxing or waning lunar phase, "crescent" or "increscent" refers to the waxing first quarter, while the symbol representing the waning final quarter is called "decrescent".

    Brand
    Crescent, originally called the Crescent Tool Company, is a brand of hand tools. Founded in 1907, the Crescent brand has changed ownership multiple times. It is currently owned by Apex Tool Group, LLC of Sparks, Maryland as part of its Hand Tools division. It is best known for its style of adjustable wrench. Wikipedia
