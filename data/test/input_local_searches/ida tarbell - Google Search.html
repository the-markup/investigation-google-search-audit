      Ida Minerva Tarbell was an American writer, investigative journalist, biographer and lecturer. She was one of the leading muckrakers of the Progressive Era of the late 19th and early 20th centuries and pioneered investigative journalism. Wikipedia
      Born: November 5, 1857, Amity Township, PA
      Died: January 6, 1944, Bridgeport Hospital, Bridgeport, CT
      Education: Titusville High School, Allegheny College
      Parents: Franklin Summer Tarbell, Esther Ann Tarbell
      Siblings: Franklin Sumner Tarbell, Jr., Will Tarbell, Sarah Tarbell
